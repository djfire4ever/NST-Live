Who are we?

At Nina’s Sweet Treats, every dessert tells a story a story of love, faith, and community.

What started as a passion for baking has grown into a faith-filled journey of sweet creations made to bring people together and celebrate life’s special moments. Rooted in the belief that food should nourish both the body and the spirit, we bake everything with purpose and heart. Our slogan, “Baked with love, built on faith,” reflects the foundation of our business: honoring God in all we do, showing love through every handcrafted treat, and serving our community with kindness and joy. Jeremiah 29:11

Why choose us?

Every creation is a labor of love, inspired by faith, family, and the joy of sharing sweet moments.

We specialize in handcrafted desserts that blend cozy flavors, beautiful detail, and a touch of grace — perfect for celebrations big and small, or simply treating yourself. From buttery cookies and frosted cupcakes to seasonal specialties and custom designs, we’re here to make life just a little sweeter — one bite at a time.
